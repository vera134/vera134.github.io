GLOWING

2024

vr game

blender

unity

team of four

Glowing is a therapeutic VR experience for players to discover a wounded mythical creature in the woods and heal it using traditional Chinese medicine practices. Through interactions like collecting herbs, crafting remedies, and feeding the creature, players embark on an emotional journey of growth, care, and exploration.

Storyboard/Narration

Considering the narrative and hardness of the game level, we initially planned three distinct settings: a mountain, an outdoor farming system, and an indoor healing and creature interaction system.

After extensive modelling and feedback, I found the story too complex for our scope. Developing multiple settings and systems left little time to refine gameplay, especially creature interactions. I simplified it to a mountain and a house, removed farming, and focused on potion-making.

Room Interior Modelling in Blender

I'm responsible for modelling the room interior, including the bedroom, workstation, and overall layout, and delivering it to Unity for interaction programming.

I aimed for a Chinese ink painting style for its traditional beauty. However, the detailed 2D artwork and matching 3D models proved too time-consuming. To maintain consistency, after discussion, I switched to a simple, low-poly 3D style while keeping a Chinese-inspired interior design.

Poster Design

I created an executive summary to present our VR game's concept, goals, and value to stakeholders like showcase attendees, evaluators, and professors. I designed it as a bifold brochure for easy display, distribute, and engage with during showcase.

Key Takeaways

  • managed team workflow and coordination as lead manager, modeler and UX designer
  • balancing ambition with available time and resources by simplifying the story and gameplay
  • understanding the VR game development process
